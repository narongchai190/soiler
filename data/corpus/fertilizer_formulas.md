## Calculation Examples / ตัวอย่างการคำนวณ

### To supply 12 kg N/rai using Urea (46-0-0):
```
Required Urea = 12 / 0.46 = 26.1 kg/rai
```

### To supply 6 kg P₂O₅/rai using 16-20-0:
```
Required 16-20-0 = 6 / 0.20 = 30 kg/rai
(This also supplies 30 × 0.16 = 4.8 kg N)
```

### To supply 6 kg K₂O/rai using MOP (0-0-60):
```
Required MOP = 6 / 0.60 = 10 kg/rai
```
