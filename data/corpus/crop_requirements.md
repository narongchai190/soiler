## Growth Stage Requirements / ความต้องการตามระยะการเจริญเติบโต

### General Pattern

| Stage | N Need | P Need | K Need |
|-------|--------|--------|--------|
| Seedling | Medium | High | Low |
| Vegetative | High | Medium | Medium |
| Flowering | Low | Medium | High |
| Fruiting | Medium | Low | High |
